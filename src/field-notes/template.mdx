Start with a short opening paragraph. This should read like a note, not a press release: what happened, why you are writing it down, and what the reader should expect.

## Section heading

Use ordinary paragraphs for the body. Keep the writing concrete. If a note is about redaction behavior, name the kind of document or pattern involved without using private client material.

## Another section heading

Add another section when the note needs it. The page template keeps the title, date, summary, body width, headings, images, and captions consistent automatically.

### Optional smaller heading

Use smaller headings sparingly. Most field notes should be short enough that two or three sections are plenty.
